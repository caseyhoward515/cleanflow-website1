Why Clean Your Dryer Vent?

Dryer lint is highly flammable and clogged vents are a common fire risk. Regular vent cleaning is widely recommended to reduce hazards.

Beyond safety, a clogged vent restricts airflow, causing your dryer to work harder. This leads to higher utility bills and a shorter lifespan for your appliance.

Signs You Need Cleaning:

  • Clothes take more than one cycle to dry
  • Dryer gets extremely hot to the touch
  • Burning smell while dryer is running
  • Vent hood flap outside doesn't open
